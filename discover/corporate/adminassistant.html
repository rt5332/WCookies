Administrative Assistant

The Administrative Assistant reports to the General Manager and is responsible for providing support to Windy City Cookies Management in order to ensure effective administrative operations. Typical duties for an Administrative Assistant include:

  • Prepare correspondence, reports and other documents, maintain office files, open and distribute mail

  • Entering Accounts Receivable invoices into QuickBook

  • Provide general support to the broader department team and organization, including coordinating department meetings and events and supporting additional leaders as needed

  • Exercise judgment and discretion with confidential information, and exhibit considerable tact, and subtlety. Handle confidential information with urgency and care
